<p><ul><li>Point IR Detectors</li><li>Open Path IR Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infrared Flammable Gas Detector market encompasses two primary types: Point IR Detectors and Open Path IR Detectors. Point IR Detectors are designed for localized monitoring, detecting gas leaks at specific locations by measuring the infrared light absorption of the gas. In contrast, Open Path IR Detectors monitor larger areas by assessing the infrared light along a path where gas may be present, offering broader coverage. Both types are essential for enhancing safety in industrial environments prone to gas leaks.</p></p>
