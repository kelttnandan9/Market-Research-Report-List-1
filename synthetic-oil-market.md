<p><p>The synthetic oil market consists primarily of two key types: synthetic blend oil and full synthetic oil. Synthetic blend oil is a mixture of conventional oil and synthetic oil, offering enhanced protection and performance at a lower cost, suitable for consumers seeking a balance between price and quality. In contrast, full synthetic oil is entirely artificially engineered, providing superior lubrication, improved oxidation resistance, and better performance in extreme temperatures, making it ideal for high-performance vehicles and demanding driving conditions.</p></p>
