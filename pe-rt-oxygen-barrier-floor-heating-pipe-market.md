<p><p>The PE-RT oxygen barrier floor heating pipe market typically includes five-layer and three-layer tubes. Five-layer tubes consist of a middle barrier layer that prevents oxygen diffusion, surrounded by two layers of polyethylene and an inner layer, enhancing durability and thermal efficiency. Three-layer tubes, while similar, have a simpler design with a barrier layer sandwiched between two polyethylene layers, offering sufficient oxygen resistance. These tubes are essential for radiant floor heating systems, ensuring longevity and optimal performance in heating applications.</p></p>
