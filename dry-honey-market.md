<p><p>The dry honey market encompasses various forms, primarily granulated honey and powdered honey. Granulated honey is crystallized honey that retains its natural sweetness and flavor, making it suitable for baking and cooking. Powdered honey is dehydrated honey, offering convenience and long shelf life; it easily dissolves in liquids and is ideal for seasoning, beverages, and snacks. Both types cater to a diverse consumer base looking for alternatives to liquid honey, appealing for their unique properties and versatility in culinary applications.</p></p>
