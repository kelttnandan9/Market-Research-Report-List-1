<p><p>The powder metal detector market encompasses three primary types: electromagnetic induction, X-ray, and microwave detection. </p><p>Electromagnetic induction detectors use electromagnetic fields to identify metal contaminants in powders and granules. X-ray detectors provide a comprehensive analysis by visualizing the internal structure of materials, effectively detecting a range of foreign objects. Microwave detection leverages microwave signals to differentiate between metal and non-metal particles, enhancing detection accuracy. Each type offers unique advantages in ensuring quality control and safety in powder processing industries.</p></p>
