<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Alignment System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toe- In / Toe-Out Alignment System</li><li>Camber Alignment System</li><li>Caster Alignment System</li><li>Four Wheel Alignment System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel alignment system market encompasses various types that ensure optimal vehicle handling and tire longevity. The Toe-In/Tow-Out alignment focuses on the angle of the wheels in relation to the vehicle's centerline, impacting steering responsiveness. The Camber alignment adjusts the tilt of the wheels inward or outward, affecting tire wear. The Caster alignment manages the angle of the steering pivot, enhancing stability. The Four-Wheel alignment aligns all wheels simultaneously, ensuring comprehensive adjustments for improved performance and safety.</p></p>
