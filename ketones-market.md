<p><ul><li>Saturated Ketone</li><li>Unsaturated Ketone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ketone market is divided into two main types: saturated and unsaturated ketones. Saturated ketones contain only single bonds between carbon atoms, resulting in a more stable structure, making them suitable for food and pharmaceutical applications. In contrast, unsaturated ketones feature one or more double bonds, offering enhanced reactivity and versatility for chemical synthesis and industrial uses. Each type serves distinct markets, with saturated ketones often used in food additives and unsaturated ketones in fine chemicals and specialty applications.</p></p>
