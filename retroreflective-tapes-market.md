<p><strong>The Retroreflective Tapes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Bead Type</li><li>Micro Prismatic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retroreflective tapes market primarily consists of two types: glass bead type and micro prismatic type. Glass bead retroreflective tapes utilize small glass beads embedded in a layer to reflect light, offering moderate visibility and durability. In contrast, micro prismatic retroreflective tapes use advanced micro-prism technology to enhance light reflection, providing superior brightness and visibility under various conditions. Both types are widely used in road signage, vehicle markings, and safety apparel to improve visibility and safety in low-light environments.</p></p>
