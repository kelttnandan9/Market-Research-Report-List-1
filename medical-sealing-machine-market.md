<p><strong>The Medical Sealing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Non-print Sealer</li><li>Continuous Print Sealer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical sealing machine market features two primary types: Continuous Non-print Sealers and Continuous Print Sealers. Continuous Non-print Sealers focus on providing a reliable sealing solution without adding printed information, making them ideal for sterile packaging where labeling is not required during the sealing process. In contrast, Continuous Print Sealers integrate printing capabilities, allowing for the direct imprinting of relevant information, such as expiration dates and lot numbers, onto the packaging during the sealing operation, enhancing traceability and compliance.</p></p>
