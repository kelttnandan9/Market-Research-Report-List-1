<p><ul><li>Accelerometer</li><li>Gyroscope</li><li>Magnetometer</li><li>Combo Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motion detector market comprises various types, including accelerometers, gyroscopes, magnetometers, and combo sensors. Accelerometers measure linear acceleration and detect motion in multiple directions. Gyroscopes sense rotational movement and orientation changes. Magnetometers detect magnetic fields, providing compass functionalities. Combo sensors integrate multiple functionalities, combining accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers to offer comprehensive motion tracking. These sensors are widely used in mobile devices, wearables, automotive applications, and smart home technologies, driving demand for precise motion detection and enhanced user experience.</p></p>
