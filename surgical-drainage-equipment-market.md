<p><ul><li>Initiative</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical drainage equipment market can be categorized into two main types: active and passive drainage systems. Active drainage systems, often referred to as initiative systems, utilize suction mechanisms to facilitate the removal of fluids from surgical sites, promoting faster recovery. In contrast, passive drainage systems rely on gravity or natural body processes to allow fluid drainage, requiring minimal intervention. Both types serve essential roles in postoperative care, helping manage potential complications and supporting healing processes effectively.</p></p>
