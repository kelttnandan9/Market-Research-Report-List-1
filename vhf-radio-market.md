<p><p>The VHF radio market is primarily divided into two types: handheld VHF radios and fixed-mount VHF radios. Handheld VHF radios are portable, battery-operated devices ideal for personal use and recreational activities, providing flexibility and mobility for users like boaters and hikers. In contrast, fixed-mount VHF radios are permanently installed on vessels and offer enhanced power, stability, and range for professional maritime communication. Both types cater to distinct user needs within boating, emergency services, and outdoor recreation sectors.</p></p>
