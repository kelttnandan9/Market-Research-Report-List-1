<p><p>Elevator proximity sensors enhance safety and efficiency by detecting the presence of individuals near elevator doors. Multi-beam door sensors use multiple infrared beams to create a coverage area, ensuring immediate detection of obstructions and preventing accidents. Ultrasonic door sensors emit sound waves to detect objects in their path, providing reliable performance even in challenging environments. Both sensor types are essential for modern elevator systems, improving user experience and adhering to safety standards while minimizing door-related incidents.</p></p>
