<p><strong>The ECG Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ECG Resting System</li><li>ECG Holter Monitoring System</li><li>ECG Stress Testing System</li><li>Cardiopulmonary Stress Testing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECG devices market comprises various types, including the ECG Resting System, which records the heart's electrical activity at rest for diagnostic purposes. The ECG Holter Monitoring System provides continuous monitoring over 24-48 hours, capturing arrhythmias and other heart conditions. The ECG Stress Testing System evaluates heart function under physical stress, aiding in cardiovascular risk assessment. Additionally, Cardiopulmonary Stress Testing Systems assess both cardiac and pulmonary function during exercise, offering insights into overall cardiovascular fitness and health status.</p></p>
