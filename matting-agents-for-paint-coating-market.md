<p><p>Matting agents are additives used in paints and coatings to reduce gloss and achieve a desired surface finish. They are primarily classified into two types: organic and inorganic matting agents. Organic matting agents typically consist of materials such as waxes, polymers, or natural substances that provide a smooth, even texture. Inorganic matting agents, on the other hand, derive from mineral sources like silica or talc, offering durability and chemical resistance. Both types enhance aesthetic appeal and performance in various applications.</p></p>
