<p><p>The fluid couplings market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Common Type**: These are standard couplings used primarily for connecting equipment, allowing for some degree of slippage to protect machinery from overload.</p><p>2. **Torque Limiting Type**: Designed to limit excessive torque, these couplings disengage under high load conditions, preventing damage to connected equipment.</p><p>3. **Speed Regulation Type**: These couplings maintain a consistent output speed, making them ideal for applications requiring precise speed control while accommodating varying loads.</p></p>
