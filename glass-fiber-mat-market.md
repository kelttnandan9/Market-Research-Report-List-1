<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Mat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet-Laid Process</li><li>Dry-Laid Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Fiber Mat market is primarily categorized into two types based on the manufacturing processes: Wet-Laid and Dry-Laid. The Wet-Laid process involves dispersing glass fibers in a water-based slurry, allowing for even distribution and bonding, resulting in a strong, flexible mat. In contrast, the Dry-Laid process uses dry glass fibers, which are layered and bonded using adhesives or heat, creating a denser, more rigid mat. Both types serve various applications in construction, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
