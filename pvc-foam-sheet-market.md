<p><strong>The PVC Foam Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cell Type</li><li>Closed Cell Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC foam sheet market is categorized into two main types: open cell and closed cell. Open cell PVC foam sheets feature interconnected air pockets, providing excellent sound absorption and flexibility, making them suitable for lightweight applications and insulation. In contrast, closed cell PVC foam sheets have sealed pockets, offering higher durability, moisture resistance, and structural integrity, which are ideal for applications in construction, automotive, and marine sectors. Each type serves distinct purposes based on their physical properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
