<p><p>The tuberculosis diagnostics market encompasses several key types. Culture-based methods involve growing Mycobacterium tuberculosis in a controlled environment, providing definitive results but requiring time. Sputum smear microscopy involves examining stained sputum samples for bacteria, offering rapid but less sensitive results. Rapid molecular tests use advanced technology to detect genetic material from the bacteria quickly, enabling faster diagnosis. Other methods may include skin tests or blood assays, each contributing uniquely to the overall landscape of TB detection and management.</p></p>
