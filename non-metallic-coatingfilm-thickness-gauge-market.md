<p><p>The non-metallic coating film thickness gauge market is divided into two primary types: wet film thickness gauges and dry film thickness gauges. Wet film thickness gauges are used to measure the thickness of coatings immediately after application, ensuring proper coverage and adherence before curing. In contrast, dry film thickness gauges assess the thickness of cured coatings, providing critical information on durability and performance. Both types are essential for quality control in various industries, including automotive, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
