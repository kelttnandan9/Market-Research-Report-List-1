<p><p>The transparent wound care dressings market is categorized into adhesive and non-adhesive wound care dressings. Adhesive dressings feature a sticky backing that firmly attaches to the skin, providing a waterproof barrier and allowing for observation of the wound without removal. Non-adhesive dressings, on the other hand, rely on secondary securing methods, minimizing trauma during changes and being suitable for wounds that need frequent inspection. Both types enhance healing by protecting the wound, reducing infection risk, and promoting a moist environment.</p></p>
