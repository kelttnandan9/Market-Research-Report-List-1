<p><p>Natural Language Processing (NLP) in the life sciences market encompasses various approaches, primarily Hybrid NLP and Statistical NLP. Hybrid NLP combines rule-based and statistical methods, allowing for improved accuracy in interpreting complex medical texts and biological data. This approach is often utilized for tasks like sentiment analysis and entity recognition within clinical notes. On the other hand, Statistical NLP relies on algorithms to analyze and model language patterns, enabling the extraction of insights from large datasets, such as research papers and patient records.</p></p>
