<p><p>The desktop spectrophotometer market includes various types tailored for specific applications. Atomic absorption spectrophotometers analyze elemental concentrations by measuring absorbed light. Fluorescence spectrophotometers detect emitted light from samples post-excitation, ideal for sensitive analyses. UV-visible spectrophotometers measure light absorption in the ultraviolet and visible spectrum, commonly used in chemical and biological assessments. Infrared spectrophotometers identify molecular structures based on infrared light absorption, while visible spectrophotometers focus solely on the visible spectrum for colorimetric analysis, serving diverse scientific fields.</p></p>
