<p><p>The plant extraction equipment market comprises three primary types: extraction equipment, concentration equipment, and separation and purification equipment. Extraction equipment is used to isolate desired compounds from plant materials through solvents or mechanical processes. Concentration equipment enhances the potency of extracts by removing solvents or reducing volume. Separation and purification equipment further refines extracts, ensuring the removal of impurities and achieving high purity levels. Together, these equipment types facilitate the efficient extraction and processing of valuable plant-derived compounds.</p></p>
