<p><p>The agricultural biopesticides market encompasses several types, each targeting specific pests and pathogens. Bioinsecticides focus on controlling insect populations using natural organisms or their byproducts. Biofungicides combat fungal diseases in crops, providing a sustainable alternative to chemical fungicides. Bionematicides target nematodes, microscopic worms that harm plant roots, while bioherbicides offer eco-friendly solutions for weed management. Together, these biopesticides promote sustainable agriculture by reducing reliance on synthetic chemicals and enhancing crop health through biologically-based approaches.</p></p>
