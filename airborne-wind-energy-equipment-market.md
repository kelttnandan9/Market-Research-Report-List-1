<p><p>The Airborne Wind Energy Equipment market features two primary types: Aerostat and Autogiro. Additionally, tethered devices, which include kites and drones, are also part of this market, using tethers to connect to generators on the ground. Together, these technologies aim to improve energy capture efficiency and expand renewable energy sources.</p></p>
