<p><p>The scleral lens market is divided into two main types: mini-scleral lenses and large-scleral lenses. Mini-scleral lenses typically have a smaller diameter, resting partially on the sclera and providing comfort for patients with minor corneal irregularities. They are ideal for individuals with less severe eye conditions. In contrast, large-scleral lenses cover a larger portion of the eye's surface, offering enhanced stability and vision correction, making them suitable for patients with more complex corneal issues or conditions like keratoconus.</p></p>
